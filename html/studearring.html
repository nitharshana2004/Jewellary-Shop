Stud Earring

Clearly identifiable, the ear stud is made by the thin pin, which is pushed through the ear hole and fixed by a counterpart. This so-called stopper, which is often also called a plug or further on a poppet, is pushed onto the pin on the back of the ear. This ensures a secure hold of the ear jewellery.
