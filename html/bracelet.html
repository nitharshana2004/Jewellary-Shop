Bracelet

A bracelet is an article of jewellery that is worn around the wrist. Bracelets may serve different uses,such as being worn as an ornament. When worn as ornaments, bracelets may have a supportive function to hold other items of decoration, such as charms. In many ancient civilizations, bracelets were considered symbolic, often used in rituals or as protection against malevolent spirits. These early bracelets served both functional and ornamental purposes.
